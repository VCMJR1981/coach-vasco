SKILL: Paddle Technique
LEVEL: 1
CATEGORY: foundations
GOAL: move efficiently through water, catch waves, conserve energy
PREREQ: —
NEXT: duck_dive, turtle_roll, paddle_for_wave

MECHANICS:
- chin lifted, chest pressed down, hips heavy on board
- full arm extension on entry, pull through hip before exit
- alternate arms, core stabilises the rotation
- legs still unless sprinting for a wave

CUES: chin_up, long_stroke, pull_past_hip
ERRORS:
- short choppy strokes / extend fully, slow the stroke rate down
- lifting hips off board / press chest down, keep hips heavy
- splashing on entry / enter fingertips first, slice not slap

CONTEXT: open water, pool, swim bench
EX: ex_pool_sprint_10s, ex_pullup_pronated, ex_swim_freestyle
EASY: ex_pullup_pronated
HARD: ex_pool_sprint_10s

SKILL: Duck Dive
LEVEL: 1
CATEGORY: foundations
GOAL: get through breaking waves without losing ground
PREREQ: paddle_technique
NEXT: pop_up

MECHANICS:
- paddle hard toward wave to build momentum
- hands press nose down as wave arrives
- knee or foot pushes tail down to angle board underwater
- body follows board through, head surfaces after tail clears

CUES: commit_early, push_nose_not_arms, stay_flat_underwater
ERRORS:
- starting too late / read the wave, start pressing before it hits
- angling board too steep / keep board angled shallow, not vertical
- popping up too soon / wait until full wave has passed over

CONTEXT: beach break, reef, any shore paddle-out
EX: ex_pool_submersion, ex_paddle_sprint
EASY: ex_pool_submersion
HARD: ex_paddle_sprint into immediate duck dive

SKILL: Turtle Roll
LEVEL: 1
CATEGORY: foundations
GOAL: hold position through breaking wave on a large board
PREREQ: paddle_technique
NEXT: pop_up

MECHANICS:
- paddle toward wave with momentum
- grip rails at chest level, roll board upside down over you
- pull board down onto back, hold breath, arms locked
- roll back up as foam passes, resume paddling immediately

CUES: grip_rails_tight, pull_board_down, roll_back_fast
ERRORS:
- letting go of board / arms must stay locked throughout
- rolling too early / time it so board inverts just before impact
- slow recovery / practise the roll-back as one fast movement

CONTEXT: any board too large to duck dive
EX: ex_pool_turtle_roll, ex_rail_grip_strength
EASY: ex_rail_grip_strength
HARD: ex_pool_turtle_roll with forward momentum

SKILL: Wave Reading & Lineup Positioning
LEVEL: 1
CATEGORY: foundations / knowledge
GOAL: choose the right position in the lineup and read which waves to catch
PREREQ: paddle_technique
NEXT: paddle_for_wave, angled_takeoff

MECHANICS:
- sit at the peak — where waves are steepest and first break
- watch the horizon: darker water = incoming swell, white lines = sets
- read direction before paddling — identify if wave will peel left, right, or close out
- right of way: surfer closest to peak has priority; never paddle in front of someone on a wave
- reposition after each wave — paddle back to peak, not to the shoulder

CUES: eyes_horizon, sit_deepest, read_before_paddling
ERRORS:
- sitting too far on shoulder / always missing the best part of the wave / paddle deeper toward the peak
- not watching incoming swells / getting caught inside / look to the horizon every 10–15 seconds
- ignoring right of way / collisions and conflict / learn who has priority before paddling for shared waves

CONTEXT: any surf break — beach break, point break, reef
EX: ex_lineup_observation_session, ex_wave_prediction_drill
EASY: ex_lineup_observation_session (watch 20 waves, predict each one before it breaks)
HARD: ex_wave_prediction_drill in busy or complex break

SKILL: Paddle for a Wave
LEVEL: 1
CATEGORY: foundations
GOAL: catch an unbroken wave by timing and committing the paddle sprint
PREREQ: paddle_technique, wave_reading
NEXT: pop_up, angled_takeoff

MECHANICS:
- turn board toward shore early — before the wave reaches you
- 2–3 powerful strokes to match wave speed, then sprint the last 3–4 strokes
- feel the wave lift the tail — that's the commit point
- add leg kick in final strokes to increase speed and catching chance
- keep chin up and chest down through the sprint

CUES: turn_early, sprint_last_3, feel_the_lift, kick_to_catch
ERRORS:
- turning too late / wave passes under you / start turning as soon as you decide to go
- paddling too slowly / not matching wave speed / hard sprint for last 4 strokes minimum
- stopping when you feel the lift / wave stalls and drops you / keep paddling until fully committed
- missing waves behind / not reading speed / two more strong strokes than you think you need

CONTEXT: any unbroken wave — beach break, reef, point
EX: ex_paddle_sprint_10s, ex_pool_sprint_10s, ex_wave_catch_reps
EASY: ex_wave_catch_reps on small gentle waves
HARD: ex_paddle_sprint_10s into immediate pop-up

SKILL: Pop-Up
LEVEL: 1
CATEGORY: foundations
GOAL: get from prone to surf stance in one explosive movement
PREREQ: paddle_technique, paddle_for_wave
NEXT: stance_trim, bottom_turn

MECHANICS:
- hands at lower chest / hip level, not shoulders
- explosive push — chest and hips rise simultaneously
- front foot lands between hands, back foot follows immediately
- land wide stance, knees bent, eyes forward

CUES: hands_low, one_motion, land_wide, eyes_up
ERRORS:
- two-step pop-up / train as one explosive action — power move not a climb
- hands too high near shoulders / weight too far back, slows movement
- feet too narrow on landing / widen stance, more stability
- looking down at board / eyes forward from the moment you push

CONTEXT: dry land, sand, gym mat
EX: ex_popup_mat, ex_popup_sprint_5reps, ex_plyopushup
EASY: ex_popup_mat slow practice
HARD: ex_popup_sprint_5reps with 10s rest

SKILL: Stance & Trim
LEVEL: 1
CATEGORY: foundations
GOAL: maintain balance and control speed while riding the wave
PREREQ: pop_up
NEXT: bottom_turn, frontside_pumping, backside_pumping

MECHANICS:
- feet hip-width or wider, front foot ~45°, back foot across tail
- knees soft and bent, weight centred between both feet
- arms low and out for balance, not raised
- shift weight forward to accelerate, back to slow or stall

CUES: stay_low, wide_stance, weight_forward_to_go, weight_back_to_slow
ERRORS:
- stiff straight legs / unlock the knees, absorb the wave
- arms too high / lower arms, relaxed and wide
- weight too far back / board bogs — move front foot forward
- looking down / chin up, eyes on the wave ahead

CONTEXT: any open wave face
EX: ex_balance_board, ex_indo_board_stance, ex_squat_hold
EASY: ex_squat_hold 60s
HARD: ex_indo_board_stance with eyes closed

SKILL: Angled Take-Off
LEVEL: 2
CATEGORY: entry / positioning
GOAL: take off already moving across the face to gain immediate speed and beat closing sections
PREREQ: pop_up, wave_reading
NEXT: trimming, frontside_bottom_turn, backside_bottom_turn

MECHANICS:
- read direction before paddling — identify left or right
- paddle mostly straight until wave is lifting, then angle last 2–3 strokes 20–45° toward shoulder
- lift into cobra as wave pushes — rotate head, chest, shoulders toward direction of travel
- pop up along the same angle, hips and gaze already down the line

CUES: decide_before_paddling, angle_last_3, cobra_and_rotate, land_down_the_line
ERRORS:
- angling too early / losing paddle power, missing the wave / paddle straight until last 3 strokes
- not angling enough / dropping to the bottom, getting closed out / turn late but clearly — 20–30°
- angling board but not body / body pulls board straight again / turn head and chest first
- pearling on angled take-offs / nose buried / lift into cobra, weight slightly back, then pop

CONTEXT: peeling beach break, point, reef — any directional wave
EX: ex_last_3_strokes_drill, ex_cobra_lean_practice, ex_beach_popup_angled
EASY: ex_cobra_lean_practice on mellow waves
HARD: ex_last_3_strokes_drill on faster steeper waves

SKILL: Trimming
LEVEL: 2
CATEGORY: speed control / line adjustment
GOAL: maintain a smooth, controlled glide in the sweet spot of the wave
PREREQ: stance_trim, angled_takeoff
NEXT: frontside_pumping, backside_pumping, frontside_bottom_turn, backside_bottom_turn

MECHANICS:
- angle the board along the wave, not straight to the beach
- stay top third to mid-face for best balance of speed and control
- lean slightly forward (front knee over front foot) to speed up
- ease weight toward back foot to slow or hold position
- micro heel/toe leans to drift up or down the face — small movements only

CUES: point_along_wave, stay_high, front_foot_accelerator, back_foot_brake, micro_leans
ERRORS:
- riding straight to beach / nose pointed to shore / point chest and board along the wave
- standing too upright and stiff / board slow and chattery / soft knees, tiny leans
- over-steering with big turns / board zigzags, loses speed / think dial not switch
- staying too low on face / stuck in weak water / climb a little higher, lean onto front foot

CONTEXT: open face, any wave with a shoulder
EX: ex_trim_laps_small_waves, ex_high_low_lines, ex_balance_board_weight_shifts
EASY: ex_trim_laps_small_waves — ride as far as possible with no big turns
HARD: ex_high_low_lines — deliberately move between high and low line using only micro-leans

SKILL: Frontside Bottom Turn
LEVEL: 2
CATEGORY: turns
GOAL: convert downward speed into drive up the wave face, setting up the next manoeuvre
PREREQ: stance_trim, angled_takeoff
NEXT: frontside_top_turn, frontside_cutback, frontside_snap, frontside_floater

MECHANICS:
- ride down the face with speed, eyes already on target (lip or high line)
- compress deeply at the lower third — knees bend, centre of gravity drops
- shift weight to inside rail and toes, lead with front arm toward target
- increase back-foot pressure as arc tightens
- extend through the turn to project up the face

CUES: eyes_to_target, compress_at_bottom, drive_front_arm, extend_to_project
ERRORS:
- turning too high on face / weak angle, no drive / wait for the lower third
- staying tall / no rail engagement / sit into knees like a squat before leaning
- over-weighting front foot / nose bogs / shift slightly to back foot as you turn
- looking at nose / board tracks straight / turn your head first, board follows eyes

CONTEXT: any open-face wave with enough drop to generate speed
EX: ex_surfskate_bottom_turn, ex_balance_board_turn, ex_popup_to_turn_drill
EASY: ex_surfskate_bottom_turn
HARD: ex_popup_to_turn_drill on steeper wave

SKILL: Backside Bottom Turn
LEVEL: 2
CATEGORY: turns
GOAL: redirect from the drop back up the wave face and set up the next backside manoeuvre
PREREQ: stance_trim, angled_takeoff
NEXT: backside_top_turn, backside_cutback, backside_snap, backside_floater

MECHANICS:
- take off near the power and angle slightly down the line
- stay low and compressed — harder to see the wave so posture matters more
- lean back onto heels to engage heelside rail, reach front hand back toward wall
- increase back-foot pressure to pivot and convert drop speed into drive up the face
- rotate hips then shoulders toward target, extend to project

CUES: stay_low, heels_to_wall, reach_front_hand_back, eyes_over_front_shoulder
ERRORS:
- turning too early / board never reaches power zone / wait for the bottom third
- going to the flats / rail disengages, slow turn / turn while still on the face
- looking at nose or whitewater / shallow turns / eyes over front shoulder to the target
- chest closed to the wave / can't rotate properly / reach front hand toward the face to open back

CONTEXT: any backside open-face wave
EX: ex_surfskate_backside_carve, ex_rail_to_rail_heelside, ex_bottom_turn_only_drill
EASY: ex_rail_to_rail_heelside
HARD: ex_bottom_turn_only_drill on steeper waves

SKILL: Frontside Top Turn
LEVEL: 2
CATEGORY: turns
GOAL: use speed from the bottom turn to redirect back down the face toward the pocket
PREREQ: frontside_bottom_turn, trimming
NEXT: frontside_snap, frontside_cutback, frontside_roundhouse

MECHANICS:
- drive into a solid bottom turn, eyes fixed on target high on the face
- extend gradually up the face, slightly lighten front leg near the top
- initiate rotation by swinging trailing arm, head and shoulders follow
- engage heelside rail, board pivots back toward beach
- absorb any lip contact, shift weight forward to re-engage and descend

CUES: eyes_to_lip, extend_up, swing_trailing_arm, absorb_and_drive_down
ERRORS:
- no bottom turn first / arriving at top with no speed, bog mid-turn / stronger bottom turn first
- turning too low on face / shallow carve, not a real top turn / wait and climb higher
- over-rotating shoulders / board slides out / bend first, feel rail bite before adding rotation
- staying too tall / bounced by lip / stay low on impact, absorb and drive back down

CONTEXT: open-face wave with clear upper third
EX: ex_surfskate_top_turn_bank, ex_hit_the_mark_drill, ex_video_review_4frames
EASY: ex_hit_the_mark_drill — pick a visual point high on face and make mellow direction change
HARD: ex_surfskate_top_turn_bank with increasing compression depth

SKILL: Frontside Cutback
LEVEL: 2
CATEGORY: turns / positioning
GOAL: redirect back to the wave's power source when too far ahead on the shoulder
PREREQ: frontside_bottom_turn, frontside_top_turn, trimming
NEXT: frontside_roundhouse, frontside_snap, frontside_floater

MECHANICS:
- stay top third of wave, notice when shoulder is flattening
- shift weight to back foot and toeside rail, rotate upper body back toward pocket
- compress to lower centre of gravity, hold rail through the arc
- front arm points back toward whitewater — board follows
- rebound off foam or pivot near pocket, shift to front foot to drive out

CUES: top_third_only, front_arm_to_pocket, compress_through_arc, front_foot_to_exit
ERRORS:
- initiating from the flats / losing all speed / start cutback from top third only
- cutting back but never reaching pocket / point front arm all the way to foam
- board skids instead of carving / bend first, then lean — feel rail bite before rotating
- no power off rebound / stay loaded through middle, then shift to front foot at foam

CONTEXT: open-face wave with clear shoulder and defined pocket
EX: ex_surfskate_carve_180, ex_cone_shoulder_to_pocket, ex_cutback_only_sessions
EASY: ex_surfskate_carve_180
HARD: ex_cone_shoulder_to_pocket at speed with rebound

SKILL: Frontside Pumping
LEVEL: 2
CATEGORY: speed generation
GOAL: generate and maintain speed between manoeuvres on the open face
PREREQ: stance_trim, trimming, frontside_bottom_turn
NEXT: frontside_snap, frontside_floater, frontside_tube

MECHANICS:
- stay top half of wave — steeper face = more energy
- compress as you move down the line toward lower part of trim line
- extend up the face, letting legs drive board back toward steeper section
- combine up-down with subtle rail-to-rail: toes toward wave climbing, neutral dropping
- front foot projects forward, back foot drives and releases tail each pump

CUES: stay_top_half, compress_to_drop, extend_to_climb, front_foot_projects, back_foot_drives
ERRORS:
- bouncing in place / only up-down, no line change / move up and down the face not just on the board
- surfing the flats / stuck in dead water / climb into top half, compress low extend high
- pumping only with front foot / nose bobs, tail drags / drive with both legs each pump
- over-steering with shoulders / board zigzags / small rail leans, quiet shoulders

CONTEXT: open face with shoulder, soft or running waves
EX: ex_small_wave_pump_runs, ex_surfskate_bank_pump, ex_flat_section_rescue
EASY: ex_surfskate_bank_pump
HARD: ex_flat_section_rescue — pump through known flat section to next steep part

SKILL: Frontside Snap / Re-entry
LEVEL: 3
CATEGORY: turns / lip manoeuvre
GOAL: explosive direction change at or near the lip, throwing spray and re-entering the face with speed
PREREQ: frontside_bottom_turn, frontside_top_turn, frontside_pumping
NEXT: frontside_roundhouse, frontside_air, frontside_tube

MECHANICS:
- approach steep section with speed, shallow bottom turn — across and up not straight up
- wind up back arm as you rise, eyes locked on exact lip spot
- at lip, release torque — swing trailing arm, lead with front shoulder and head
- extend back leg sharply to pivot board off tail, body stays low and compressed
- snap back toward face, stamp front foot to catch and re-engage rail

CUES: shallow_bottom_turn, wind_back_arm, eyes_on_lip_spot, karate_kick_back_leg, front_foot_catch
ERRORS:
- getting pitched / bottom turn too deep / shallower turn, hit earlier — across and up
- weak flat snap / hitting soft section / wind the back arm, hit steepest part, push hard off tail
- board slides too far / not enough front foot / snap then stamp front foot to bring nose back
- bouncing off lip / no compression / bend before impact, stay low through the hit

CONTEXT: steep pitching section or soft closeout, chest high and above
EX: ex_surfskate_snap_cone, ex_explosive_squat_rotation, ex_soft_closeout_snap_drill
EASY: ex_soft_closeout_snap_drill on small waves
HARD: ex_surfskate_snap_cone at speed with full rotation

SKILL: Frontside Floater

SKILL: Frontside Roundhouse Cutback
LEVEL: 3
CATEGORY: advanced rail turn / full direction change
GOAL: carve 180°+ back to whitewater, rebound off foam, and return to open face with speed
PREREQ: frontside_cutback, frontside_top_turn
NEXT: frontside_snap, frontside_tube

MECHANICS:
- race down soft shoulder with speed, stay top third
- shallow bottom turn back up, shift to back foot and heelside rail
- swing back arm around front arm, rotate shoulders to start long carve toward pocket
- hold rail and compression through 180°, eyes locked on foam target
- absorb foam with soft knees, shift weight down wave and onto toeside for return
- tighter snap back to face, front foot drives projection down the line

CUES: top_third_with_speed, heels_to_wrap, eyes_on_foam, absorb_not_smash, toes_to_return
ERRORS:
- bogging before foam / not enough speed or too low / start higher and carry more speed in
- looks like a normal cutback / not completing 180° / wrap past 180° and commit to the foam
- getting blasted by rebound / staying stiff / stay low, let foam lift you, shift weight down the wave quickly
- arcs feel disconnected / think big 8 shape — long wrap out, tight snap back

CONTEXT: soft tapered shoulder with room for a big arc
EX: ex_surfskate_figure8_cones, ex_small_wave_roundhouse_reps, ex_video_4checkpoints
EASY: ex_surfskate_figure8_cones
HARD: ex_small_wave_roundhouse_reps — commit to full wrap and foam hit every wave

PREREQ: backside_cutback, backside_top_turn
NEXT: backside_snap, backside_tube
